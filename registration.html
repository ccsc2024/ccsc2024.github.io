Registration



All participants, including keynote and invited speakers, must register via an online application portal.
The registration fee includes access to the conference venue and sessions, conference materials, morning and afternoon coffee breaks, and drinks and light refreshments during poster sessions.

Registration is a two-step process:

(1) create an account and accept the CCSC 2024 Code of Conduct,
(2) register for the conference and pay the registration fee.

Once you complete these steps, you will receive a confirmation email and will be able to access the registration and payment confirmation in the online application portal.

Abstracts



All participants are encouraged to submit an abstract and present their research in one of the following formats:



  • Talk (invited speakers)
  • Poster
  • Digital Demo (showcase software / GUI / webtool / etc.)


Note that there is a limited number of slots for contributed talks and digital demos; if you apply for either of these and are not selected, you will automatically be considered for a poster presentation. Submission deadline is March 15, 2024. Please prepare your abstract using this Word template, and submit it as DOC, DOCX, or PDF via our abstract submission portal.
